# AWSome Bank - Retail Banking FAQ

## Account Types

### What types of accounts does AWSome Bank offer?
AWSome Bank offers the following account types:
- **Everyday Checking**: No minimum balance, $5/month fee (waived with direct deposit). Includes free debit card and mobile banking.
- **Premium Checking**: $1,500 minimum balance, no monthly fee. Earns 0.5% APY, includes free checks and wire transfers.
- **Savings Account**: $100 minimum to open, earns 1.2% APY. Limited to 6 withdrawals per month.
- **High-Yield Savings**: $5,000 minimum balance, earns 4.1% APY. Online-only account.
- **Certificate of Deposit (CD)**: Terms from 3 months to 5 years. Rates from 3.5% to 5.0% APY depending on term.

### What are the CD rates by term?
| Term | APY | Minimum Deposit |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| 3 months | 3.5% | $1,000 |
| 6 months | 3.9% | $1,000 |
| 12 months | 4.25% | $1,000 |
| 24 months | 4.5% | $1,000 |
| 36 months | 4.75% | $2,500 |
| 60 months | 5.0% | $5,000 |

Early withdrawal penalty: 90 days of interest for CDs under 12 months, 180 days for 12 months or longer. CD interest can be paid monthly to your checking or savings account, or reinvested into the CD at maturity.

### How do I open a new account?
You can open an account online at awsomebank.com, through our mobile app, or by visiting any branch. You will need a valid government-issued ID, Social Security number, and an initial deposit. Online applications typically take 10-15 minutes.

### What is the minimum balance for each account?
- Everyday Checking: No minimum balance required
- Premium Checking: $1,500 minimum to avoid fees
- Savings Account: $100 minimum to open
- High-Yield Savings: $5,000 minimum balance
- CD: $1,000 minimum deposit

## Fees and Charges

### What are the monthly fees?
- Everyday Checking: $5/month (waived with direct deposit of $500+)
- Premium Checking: No monthly fee with $1,500 minimum balance; $15/month otherwise
- Savings Account: No monthly fee
- High-Yield Savings: No monthly fee with $5,000 minimum balance; $10/month otherwise

### What are the overdraft fees?
AWSome Bank charges $30 per overdraft transaction. You can opt into Overdraft Protection which links your savings account to cover shortfalls for a $10 transfer fee instead. We also offer a $50 overdraft grace amount at no charge.

### Are there ATM fees?
- AWSome Bank ATMs: Free for all account holders
- Partner network ATMs (AllPoint): Free for Premium Checking holders
- Out-of-network ATMs: $2.50 per transaction for Everyday Checking; free for Premium Checking
- International ATMs: $3.00 per transaction plus 1% foreign transaction fee

## Transfers and Payments

### How do I transfer money between accounts?
You can transfer money through online banking, the mobile app, by phone, or at a branch. Internal transfers between AWSome Bank accounts are instant and free. External transfers via ACH take 1-3 business days.

### What are the wire transfer fees?
- Domestic incoming wire: Free
- Domestic outgoing wire: $25 (free for Premium Checking)
- International incoming wire: $15
- International outgoing wire: $45

### Does AWSome Bank support Zelle?
Yes, Zelle is integrated into our mobile app and online banking. You can send and receive money using an email address or phone number. Transfers between Zelle users are typically completed within minutes.

## Cards and Security

### What do I do if my debit card is lost or stolen?
Immediately lock your card through the mobile app or call our 24/7 hotline at 1-800-555-BANK. You can request a replacement card online or at any branch. Replacement cards are typically delivered within 5-7 business days, or you can get an instant-issue card at select branches.

### Does AWSome Bank offer credit cards?
Yes, we offer three credit cards:
- **AWSome Rewards Card**: 1.5% cash back on all purchases, no annual fee
- **AWSome Travel Card**: 2x points on travel and dining, $95 annual fee
- **AWSome Platinum Card**: 2% cash back on everything, $195 annual fee, includes airport lounge access

## Digital Banking

### What features does the mobile app offer?
The AWSome Bank mobile app includes: account balances and transaction history, mobile check deposit, bill pay, Zelle transfers, card lock/unlock, branch and ATM locator, spending insights, and push notifications for transactions.

### Is online banking secure?
Yes, AWSome Bank uses 256-bit encryption, multi-factor authentication, biometric login (fingerprint and face recognition), and real-time fraud monitoring. We also offer transaction alerts and the ability to instantly lock your card from the app.

## Loans and Mortgages

### What loan products does AWSome Bank offer?
- **Personal Loans**: $1,000 to $50,000, rates from 6.99% APR, terms 12-60 months
- **Auto Loans**: Up to $100,000, rates from 4.49% APR, terms 24-84 months
- **Home Equity Line of Credit (HELOC)**: Up to 85% LTV, variable rates from 7.5% APR
- **Mortgage**: 15-year and 30-year fixed, rates from 6.25% APR; ARM options available

### How do I apply for a loan?
You can apply online, through the mobile app, or at any branch. You will need proof of income, employment verification, and consent for a credit check. Most personal loan decisions are made within 1 business day.
