# Orders and Shipping Guide

## Placing an Order

### How to Order
1. Browse products and add items to your cart
2. Click the cart icon to review your selections
3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
4. Enter or confirm your shipping address
5. Select your preferred shipping method
6. Enter payment information
7. Review order summary and click "Place Order"

### Payment Methods Accepted
- Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit Cards: All major banks
- PayPal
- AcmeShop Gift Cards
- Apple Pay and Google Pay

### Order Confirmation
After placing your order, you will receive:
- Immediate on-screen confirmation with order number
- Email confirmation within 5 minutes
- SMS notification (if opted in)

Save your order number for tracking and customer support inquiries.

## Order Tracking

### How to Track Your Order

**Option 1: AcmeShop Website**
1. Log in to your AcmeShop account
2. Go to "My Orders"
3. Click on the order you want to track
4. View real-time tracking information

**Option 2: Tracking Link**
1. Open your shipping confirmation email
2. Click the tracking link provided
3. View carrier tracking page

**Option 3: Carrier Website**
1. Copy your tracking number from the confirmation email
2. Visit the carrier website (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
3. Enter your tracking number

### Tracking Status Definitions
- **Order Received**: We've received your order
- **Processing**: Order is being prepared
- **Shipped**: Package has left our warehouse
- **In Transit**: Package is on its way
- **Out for Delivery**: Package will arrive today
- **Delivered**: Package has been delivered

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Received

If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received your package:

1. **Check around your property**: Look at back doors, porches, garages, or with neighbors
2. **Wait 24 hours**: Sometimes tracking updates before actual delivery
3. **Check with household members**: Someone else may have received it
4. **Contact your local post office or carrier**: They may have more information

If still not found after 48 hours, contact AcmeShop customer support with your order number.

### Delayed Shipment

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery date:

1. Check tracking for any updates or exceptions
2. Weather events, holidays, or carrier issues may cause delays
3. Contact us if the delay exceeds 5 business days past the estimate

### Damaged Package

If your package arrives damaged:

1. Take photos of the damaged packaging and contents
2. Do not discard the packaging
3. Contact AcmeShop customer support within 48 hours
4. We will arrange a replacement or refund

### Wrong Item Received

If you received the wrong item:

1. Do not open or use the incorrect item
2. Contact customer support with your order number
3. We will send a prepaid return label
4. The correct item will be shipped immediately

### Missing Items from Order

If items are missing from your order:

1. Check if your order was split into multiple shipments (check email)
2. Review your order confirmation to verify items ordered
3. Contact customer support if items are confirmed missing
