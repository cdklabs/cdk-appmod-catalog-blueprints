# Returns and Refunds Policy

## Return Policy Overview

AcmeShop offers a hassle-free return policy to ensure your complete satisfaction.

### Return Window
- **Standard Items**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Electronics**: 15 days from delivery date
- **Holiday Purchases** (Nov 1 - Dec 31): Extended returns until January 31

### Return Conditions

Items must be:
- In original, unused condition
- In original packaging with all tags attached
- Accompanied by proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt)

### Non-Returnable Items
The following items cannot be returned:
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Intimate apparel and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
- Opened software, music, or video games
- Gift cards
- Hazardous materials
- Items marked "Final Sale"

## How to Return an Item

### Online Return Process

1. **Log in** to your AcmeShop account
2. **Go to "My Orders"** and find the order containing the item
3. **Click "Return Item"** next to the product
4. **Select return reason** from the dropdown menu
5. **Choose return method**:
   - Drop off at UPS/FedEx location (free)
   - Schedule home pickup ($5.99 fee)
6. **Print return label** and packing slip
7. **Pack the item** securely in original packaging
8. **Attach return label** to the outside of the package
9. **Drop off** at designated location or wait for pickup

### In-Store Returns

If you have an AcmeShop retail location nearby:
1. Bring the item with proof of purchase
2. Visit the customer service desk
3. Receive immediate refund or exchange

### Return Shipping

- **Defective or Wrong Items**: Free return shipping
- **Change of Mind**: Free drop-off at carrier locations, or $5.99 for home pickup
- **International Returns**: Customer responsible for return shipping costs

## Refund Process

### Refund Timeline

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Credit Card | 5-7 business days after we receive the return |
| Debit Card | 5-10 business days after we receive the return |
| PayPal | 3-5 business days after we receive the return |
| Gift Card | 1-2 business days (refunded to gift card balance) |
| Store Credit | Immediate upon return processing |

### Refund Amount

Your refund will include:
- Full purchase price of returned item(s)
- Original shipping cost (only if entire order is returned due to our error)

Your refund will NOT include:
- Original shipping cost (for change of mind returns)
- Return shipping fees (if applicable)

### Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:
- Items returned after 30 days but within 60 days (15% restocking fee)
- Items with missing parts or accessories
- Items showing signs of use or wear

### Refund Notification

You will receive:
- Email confirmation when your return is received
- Email notification when refund is processed
- Refund will appear on your statement within the timeline above

## Exchange Policy

### How to Exchange an Item

**Option 1: Return and Reorder**
1. Initiate a return for the original item
2. Place a new order for the desired item
3. Fastest way to get your new item

**Option 2: Direct Exchange**
1. Contact customer support
2. Request an exchange for a different size, color, or item
3. We'll send the new item and a return label for the original
4. Note: Direct exchanges may take longer

### Exchange Availability
- Exchanges are subject to product availability
- If the desired item is out of stock, you'll receive a full refund
- Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly

### Exchange Shipping
- Standard exchanges: Free shipping for the new item
- Expedited exchange shipping available for additional cost

## Special Circumstances

### Defective Products

If you receive a defective product:
1. Contact customer support within 48 hours of delivery
2. Provide photos of the defect
3. We will send a replacement immediately with free expedited shipping
4. Return the defective item using our prepaid label

### Warranty Claims

For items within warranty period:
1. Contact customer support with proof of purchase
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. We will coordinate repair or replacement
4. See product documentation for specific warranty terms

### Gift Returns

If you received an item as a gift:
1. You can return it for store credit (no receipt needed)
2. For refund to original payment, the purchaser must initiate the return
3. Gift receipts can be used for exchanges or store credit
