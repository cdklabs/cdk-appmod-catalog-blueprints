# Account Help Guide

## Creating an Account

### Registration Process

1. **Visit acmeshop.com** and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
2. **Enter your information**:
   - Email address (will be your username)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, must include number and special character)
   - First and last name
   - Phone number (optional, for order updates)
3. **Verify your email**: Click the link sent to your email address
4. **Complete your profile**: Add shipping address and payment methods (optional)

### Account Benefits

With an AcmeShop account, you can:
- Track orders and view order history
- Save multiple shipping addresses
- Store payment methods for faster checkout
- Create wishlists and save items for later
- Receive personalized recommendations
- Access exclusive member-only deals
- Earn and redeem AcmeRewards points

### Guest Checkout

Don't want to create an account? You can:
- Check out as a guest for one-time purchases
- Track your order using the order number and email
- Note: Guest orders don't earn AcmeRewards points

## Password Management

### Password Requirements

Your password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include at least one uppercase letter
- Include at least one lowercase letter
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

### How to Reset Your Password

**If you forgot your password:**

1. Go to acmeshop.com and click "Sign In"
2. Click "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter the email address associated with your account
4. Check your email for a password reset link (arrives within 5 minutes)
5. Click the link and enter your new password
6. Confirm your new password
7. You'll be automatically logged in

**Note**: Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If expired, request a new one.

**If you know your current password:**

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Account Settings" > "Security"
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter and confirm your new password
6. Click "Save Changes"

### Account Security Tips

- Use a unique password not used on other websites
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra security
- Never share your password with anyone
- Log out when using shared or public computers
- Review your account activity regularly

## Profile Management

### Updating Personal Information

To update your name, email, or phone number:

1. Log in to your AcmeShop account
2. Click your name in the top right corner
3. Select "Account Settings"
4. Click "Personal Information"
5. Update the desired fields
6. Click "Save Changes"

**Note**: Changing your email requires verification of the new email address.

### Managing Shipping Addresses

**To add a new address:**
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Addresses"
2. Click "Add New Address"
3. Enter the address details
4. Optionally set as default shipping address
5. Click "Save Address"

**To edit an existing address:**
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Addresses"
2. Click "Edit" next to the address
3. Make your changes
4. Click "Save Changes"

**To delete an address:**
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Addresses"
2. Click "Delete" next to the address
3. Confirm deletion

You can save up to 10 shipping addresses.

### Managing Payment Methods

**To add a payment method:**
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Payment Methods"
2. Click "Add Payment Method"
3. Enter card details or link PayPal
4. Click "Save"

**To remove a payment method:**
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Payment Methods"
2. Click "Remove" next to the payment method
3. Confirm removal

**Security Note**: We never store your full card number. Only the last 4 digits are displayed.

## Notification Preferences

### Email Notifications

Manage your email preferences at "Account Settings" > "Notifications":

| Notification Type | Description | Default |
|-------------------|-------------|---------|
| Order Updates | Shipping and delivery notifications | On (required) |
| Promotional Emails | Sales, deals, and new arrivals | On |
| AcmeRewards Updates | Points balance and rewards | On |
| Product Recommendations | Personalized suggestions | On |
| Back in Stock Alerts | Notifications for wishlisted items | On |
| Newsletter | Weekly newsletter with tips and trends | Off |

### SMS Notifications

Enable text message notifications for:
- Order shipped notifications
- Delivery day reminders
- Exclusive flash sale alerts

To enable SMS:
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Notifications"
2. Toggle "SMS Notifications" on
3. Verify your phone number
4. Select which notifications to receive via SMS

**Note**: Standard messaging rates may apply.

### Push Notifications (Mobile App)

If using the AcmeShop mobile app:
1. Open the app and go to Settings
2. Tap "Notifications"
3. Toggle individual notification types on/off

## Account Troubleshooting

### Can't Log In

If you're having trouble logging in:
1. Verify you're using the correct email address
2. Check that Caps Lock is off
3. Try resetting your password
4. Clear your browser cache and cookies
5. Try a different browser or device

### Account Locked

Your account may be temporarily locked after:
- 5 failed login attempts
- Suspicious activity detected

To unlock:
1. Wait 30 minutes and try again, OR
2. Click "Forgot Password?" to reset and unlock, OR
3. Contact customer support

### Email Not Received

If you're not receiving emails from AcmeShop:
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Add noreply@acmeshop.com to your contacts
3. Verify your email address in account settings
4. Check if emails are being filtered by your email provider

### Deleting Your Account

To permanently delete your AcmeShop account:
1. Go to "Account Settings" > "Privacy"
2. Click "Delete Account"
3. Enter your password to confirm
4. Click "Permanently Delete"

**Warning**: This action cannot be undone. You will lose:
- Order history access
- Saved addresses and payment methods
- AcmeRewards points balance
- Wishlists and saved items
