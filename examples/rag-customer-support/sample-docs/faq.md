# Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders & Checkout

### How do I place an order?
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can check out as a guest or create an account for faster future purchases and order tracking.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and AcmeShop gift cards.

### Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact customer support immediately if you need to make changes.

### How do I apply a promo code?
Enter your promo code in the "Promo Code" field during checkout and click "Apply". The discount will be reflected in your order total before payment.

### Why was my order cancelled?
Orders may be cancelled due to payment issues, item unavailability, or suspected fraud. You'll receive an email explaining the reason. Contact support if you believe this was an error.

## Shipping & Delivery

### How long does shipping take?
- Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
- Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
- Next Day Delivery: Next business day
- International: 7-28 business days depending on destination

### Is shipping free?
Standard shipping is free on orders over $50. Express shipping is free on orders over $150.

### How do I track my order?
Log in to your account and go to "My Orders", or use the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to Canada, Mexico, Europe, Asia Pacific, and most other countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination.

### Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
Address changes can only be made within 1 hour of placing your order. Contact customer support immediately if you need to update your address.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Electronics have a 15-day return window. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

### How do I return an item?
Log in to your account, go to "My Orders", select the item to return, print the return label, and drop off at any UPS or FedEx location.

### How long does it take to get a refund?
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The refund will appear on your original payment method.

### Do I have to pay for return shipping?
Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. For other returns, drop-off at carrier locations is free, or home pickup is $5.99.

### Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
Yes, you can request an exchange for a different size, color, or item. Contact customer support or initiate an exchange through your account.

## Products & Availability

### How do I know if an item is in stock?
Stock status is displayed on each product page. "In Stock" items ship within 1-2 business days. "Limited" items ship within 3-5 days.

### Can I be notified when an out-of-stock item is available?
Yes, click "Notify Me" on the product page and enter your email. You'll receive an alert when the item is back in stock.

### Are your products covered by warranty?
Yes, all products include manufacturer warranty: Electronics (1 year), Home & Kitchen (2 years), Clothing (90 days), Sports & Outdoors (1 year).

### Do you price match?
We offer price matching for identical items sold by major retailers. Contact customer support with proof of the lower price within 14 days of purchase.

## Account & Security

### How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" on our website, enter your email and create a password, then verify your email address.

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in page, enter your email, and follow the link sent to your inbox to create a new password.

### How do I update my account information?
Log in and go to "Account Settings" to update your personal information, addresses, payment methods, and notification preferences.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and never store your full card number. We are PCI DSS compliant.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to "Account Settings" > "Privacy" > "Delete Account". Note that this permanently removes your order history and AcmeRewards points.

## AcmeRewards Program

### What is AcmeRewards?
AcmeRewards is our loyalty program. Earn 1 point per dollar spent, and redeem points for discounts on future purchases.

### How do I earn points?
Points are automatically earned on every purchase made with your AcmeShop account. Guest checkouts do not earn points.

### How do I redeem points?
During checkout, you'll see an option to apply your points. 100 points = $1 off your order.

### Do points expire?
Points expire 12 months after they are earned if there is no account activity. Make a purchase to keep your points active.
